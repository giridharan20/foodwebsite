About us

The FOODKING Hotel’s roots can be traced all the way back to 1957. Founded by Mr. Tony stark under the name Jarvis Hotel in Dindigul. His Restaurant was the food king of that region, which over the years became synonymous with his brand and cooking styles, leading him to earn the nick name “FOOD KING” which would eventually became the name of our brand and restaurants.
